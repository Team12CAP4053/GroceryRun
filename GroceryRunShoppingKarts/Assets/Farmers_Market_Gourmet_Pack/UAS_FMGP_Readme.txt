FARMERS' MARKET GOURMET PACK V1.0


This extremely affordable package contains over 140 farm items and prefab combinations made with love and care!

The models are between 56 and 336 polygons.

The package comes with 256x256, 512x512 and 1024x1024 textures, both diffuse and normal maps. There are also prefabs for all the different texture resolutions to make life easier for you. All the models are available as FBX files.

This package is updated every now and then and it's price will go up, but everyone who already owns the package will of course get all updates for free.
